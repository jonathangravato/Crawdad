Meet our Pros:

Phil Kitsch, Wes Doka, Craig Barr & Head Pro, Neil Roberts (left to right)

Neil Roberts - Class "A" Head Professional Neil was only five when he picked up a club for the first time. Neil became a member of the Professional Golfers Assiciation of Canada in 1977 and went on to win the BCPGA Championship in 1995. Starting as an assistant in the Pitt Meadows Golf Club in 1985, Neil worked hard and earned the name of Head Professional in 2002.

Craig Barr - Class "A" Associate Professional Born in the land of golf, Craig moved to Port Coquitlam in 1988. He started in the back shop at Pitt Meadows Golf Club in 1994 and quickly moved in the Pro Shop while he obtained his Professional Golf Management certification from Camosun College. Craig was an active member of the National College Championship Camosun College Golf Team and NWACC All Conference Team. A member of CPGA since 2002, Craig enjoys teaching all skill levels of golf and helping players to advance their game

Wes Doka - Class "A" Associate Professional Born and raised in the Lower Mainland, Wes got his start in golf at Hazelmere Golf Club as a Junior. Before finding his home at Pitt Meadows Golf Club in 2007 he worked in the shop at Frazerview Golf Course. Wes is a CPGA Professional and enjoys teaching golf to golfers of all ages and skills.

Phil Kitsch - Class "A" Associate Professional Growing up in Merrit, BC, Phil found his love for golf while working at Merrit Golf Club. 18 years later Phil still loves the game and enjoys teaching as a CPGA Professional . He has been with Pitt Meadows Golf Club for 10 years and enjoys helping others find a love for the game.ÿ

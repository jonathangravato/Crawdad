Courses

Auburn Valley Golf Club:

The 18-hole course at the Auburn Valley Golf Club facility in Auburn, California features 6,831 yards of golf from the longest tees for a par of 72. The course rating is 72.7 and it has a slope rating of 129 on Bermuda grass. Designed by Bob Bissett/Larry Curtola, the Auburn Valley golf course opened in 1960. Click here for Online Tee Times

Ancil Hoffman:

Ancil Hoffman Golf Course was designed and built in 1965 by noted architect William Bell. It is owned by the County of Sacramento and named for former County Supervisor Ancil Hoffman.
Click here for Online Tee Times

Cherry Island:

Cherry Island, one of the County of Sacramento's three championship golf courses, opened for public play on June 1, 1990. The course was designed by Robert Muir Graves to challenge golfers of every ability level.
Click here for Online Tee Times

Bidwell Park

Bidwell Park Golf Course is one of Northern California's original municipal golf courses. From the natural wilderness of upper bidwell park stems a picturesque 18 hole layout, with the front nine offering a scenic parkland setting, while the back nine is set in the foothills of Bidwell Park.Click here for Online Tee Times

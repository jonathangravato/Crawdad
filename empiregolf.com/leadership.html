    Senior Manager, Golf Shop and Retail Operationsÿ Dane Bohrer is the Facility Manager of the Ancil Hoffman and Cherry Island Golf Courses, as well as consults and advises on Golf Shop and Retail Operations at all Empire properties.ÿ Dane has been a member of the Empire Golf management team since 2005. Dane got his start as the merchandiser at Cherry Island Golf Course, where he increased sales over 200% in two years. As the Facility Manager at Cherry Island, Dane spent a great deal of time on the lesson tee and rediscovered his passion, "teaching golf." Dane graduated from the University of New Mexico with a degree in business but learned the golf business from the ground up, "in the trenches", if you will. During the golf explosion of the 80's and 90's Dane owned two golf retail stores in the Sacramento area. His genuine approach to sales and customer service is refreshing when you consider the amount of bad information the shopper is faced with in today's competitive market. Dane is a native of Dayton, Ohio and currently resides in Sacramento with his wife Lisa.

  • CALLIPPE PRESERVE GOLF COURSE PROJECTCITY OF PLEASANTON

    PLEASANTON, CALIFORNIA Dates of Service: January 1998 to December 2003 Empire Golf served as the consultant for facilityy design, operations, RFP development, management company selection process, and management contract for the operation of the Callippe Preserve Golf Course for the City of Pleasanton from January of 1998 until December 2003. The City of Pleasanton constructed a 6,700-yard, par 72, championship golf course with accompanying amenities.ÿ Empire Golf worked in conjunction with City staff and citizenry in all aspects of facility design, as well as the process of selecting a competent operator for the project. Due to Empire Golf\x{2019}s portfolio of properties in 1998, and potential conflict of interest issues as a consultant to the City of Pleasanton, Empire Golf agreed to abstain from submitting a proposal for management of the completed facility as a term of its consulting agreement.
